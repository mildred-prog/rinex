About Rinex Shine

Our Story

Rinex Shine started as a small mobile car detailing service in Nottingham, founded with a passion for delivering exceptional cleaning results right at our customers' doorsteps.

Today, we've grown to offer comprehensive cleaning services including home cleaning, office cleaning, upholstery care, and move-in/out cleaning across Nottinghamshire and surrounding areas.

Our team of professional, insured cleaners is committed to eco-friendly practices and customer satisfaction. We bring all equipment and supplies to provide convenient, high-quality service.

Whether you need your car detailed, home deep-cleaned, or office maintained, Rinex Shine delivers the same exceptional quality and attention to detail that built our reputation.
